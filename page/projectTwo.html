My certificate of completion

The opportunity came to me in one of the many faculty emails sent out to UTS students. After sending in my details, filling forms and going through many interviews, I, only with 3 others from UTS were accepted into the program

I had originally been put into a project where we create a chess AI and I was pretty excited.
