Recommendation System using Apriori

1st Internship at Komatsu Limited

Published March 16, 2022

My very first internship was at Komatsu Limited, a Japanese multinational corporation. They manufacture construction, mining and military equipment as well as a host of other things. I was dubbed the “Engineering Intern”. And you might have thought, “oh, construction equipment and engineering degree? He must be a civil engineer” however if you read my about me section, you would know that I major in data engineering.

This recommenation system that I created was the culmination of 6 months of working at Komatsu, an enjoyable experience when I look back on it. Writing all of it in Python with the help of Jupyter, Anaconda and the Pandas library, I first pulled data from the mySQL servers and copied them into csv files. Cleaning this data took the most amount of time, but such is the life of a data engineer. I then created the recommenation system using the Apriori Algorithm to compare how each item sells against each other, all while using command prompt as my UI.
